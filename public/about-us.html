About Us

We are a group of IIT Kanpur alumni trying to add value to Indian students life. The first product that we are launching is that of a chatbot which helps students prepare for aptitude tests. We provide mini mock test on the facebook chat and messenger. Aptitude tests are common test format and is used starting from MBA entrance to campus placements to many other exams. Our test consists of 10 questions and the student needs to solve them in 15 mins. A single test costs just Rs5. After the test, score of the student is communicated with detailed solution to each question. This is a innovative way to prepare for aptitude exams and sharpen once skills. We are also entering the skill building space. We are also offering classroom and workshop coaching for 10 class pass studnts in the field of fire safety, welding and electric fitter job. We provide jobs for these students in their respective fields. Please contact us to know more about these courses.
